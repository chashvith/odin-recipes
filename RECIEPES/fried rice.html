fried rice

ingredients

  • Cooked Rice (preferably cold, day-old rice works best)
  • Oil (for frying)
  • Onions (finely chopped)
  • Carrots (julienned or finely chopped)
  • Green Beans (chopped)
  • Capsicum (bell peppers, chopped)
  • Green peas
  • Ginger (finely chopped or grated)
  • Garlic (finely chopped or minced)
  • Spring Onions (chopped, for garnish)
  • Soy Sauce
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Black Pepper (to taste)
  • Sesame Oil (optional, for extra flavor)
  • Chili Sauce (optional, for heat)

steps to make

  1. Heat oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add chopped ginger and garlic, saut√© for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Add chopped onions, carrots, green beans, bell peppers, and peas. Stir-fry for 3-4 minutes until the vegetables are tender but still crisp.
  4. Add the cold cooked rice to the pan, breaking up any clumps with a spatula.
  5. Pour soy sauce over the rice and stir to evenly coat the rice and vegetables.
  6. Season with salt and black pepper to taste. Optionally, add chili sauce for extra heat.
  7. Continue to stir-fry the rice for 2-3 more minutes until heated through and well-mixed.
  8. Drizzle a little sesame oil for extra flavor (optional).
  9. Garnish with chopped spring onions.
  10. Serve hot and enjoy your Veg Fried Rice!
